🏆 From Benchwarmer to Ballon d’Or: The Rise of Raphinha

From warming the bench to lighting up the Ballon d’Or stage — this is Raphinha’s redemption arc!”

By The Nutmeg FC | 27 July 2025 | 11:28am IST

  arnav8306@gmail.com


There was a time when Raphinha was just a name on the team sheet — sometimes not even that. Doubts, dips in form, and dazzling competition at Barcelona kept him dancing on the edge of greatness. But in 2025, Raphinha didn’t just rise. He soared.



🎯 A Humble Beginning


Born in Porto Alegre, Brazil, Raphinha’s journey was never destined to be easy. He was the kid playing barefoot on muddy pitches, dreaming of the Seleção jersey. Before the spotlight found him, he was grafting hard in clubs like Vitória Guimarães, Rennes, and Leeds United — winning hearts with his passion and sheer hustle.



🔥 The Barcelona Struggle


When he joined Barcelona in 2022, fans expected magic. But reality was harsher — inconsistent game time, stiff competition from Dembele and later Lamine Yamal, and criticism for not delivering when it mattered.
He became a meme, a punchline, a "benchwarmer." But Raphinha didn’t clap back. He trained harder. He studied tapes.
He grew — mentally and tactically.



💫 The 2024-25 Explosion


The turning point? The new Barcelona Manager Hansi Flick put his faith in him again when injuries rocked the squad.
And Raphinha delivered fireworks:

  • 27 goals and 18 assists across all competitions
  • Champions League knockout heroics vs Bayern and Inter
  • Equalling Ronaldo's Champions League Record
  • A screamer in El Clásico that silenced the Bernabéu
Suddenly, the footballing world took notice. Pundits couldn’t stop praising his positional awareness, rapid acceleration, and lethal left foot. Fans started claiming him to be the next golden ball winner.



🥇 Ballon d’Or Contender?


When the Ballon d’Or shortlist dropped — Raphinha’s name was right there, and no one was laughing anymore. In a world dominated by Mbappé and Haaland, Raphinha carved his own lane with consistency, character, and chaos on the pitch.He didn’t just play for the fans — he fought for them.



📣 What Fans Are Saying:


"From Leeds to this? Raphinha’s story is unreal."
"He’s Brazil’s next great winger. Neymar would be proud."
"He proved the haters wrong. RESPECT."
